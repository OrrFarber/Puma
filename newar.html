LA LOVE STORY

CREATED BY LAUREN LONDON

This is Lauren London’s L.A. Love Story. Each piece of the collection is inspired by the city that raised her. Created in collaboration with PUMA, the collection is a mix of timeless streetwear infused with Lauren’s effortless style
